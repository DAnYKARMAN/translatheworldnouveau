How it works

Thank you for choosing TranslateTheWorld.

You are the host of the conference.
That means: you are the organizer.

You are the one who:
  • 🎬 starts the conference
  • 🎤 speaks first
  • 👥 gives the floor to participants
  • 🛑 ends the conference

1 Your two links

In your email, you have two links. They are different.

🟢
The Host link It's yours. Keep it for yourself.
Use it to start the conference.
🟠
The Participants link It's for your guests.
Send it to them before the conference by:
📧 Email 💬 WhatsApp 📱 SMS 📷 QR code

2 Start the conference

  1. Click on your Host link.
  2. Choose the language you will speak in.
  3. The session starts.
  4. Share the link with your participants by email, SMS, WhatsApp or QR code by clicking Share at the top of your speaker interface.
  5. Speak normally.
  6. Participants read the subtitles in their own language, on their phone.

3 Give the floor to a participant

During the conference, a participant may want to speak.

How they ask:

  • ✋ On their screen, they tap Raise hand
  • 🔔 Their request appears on your screen

You decide:

  • ✅ To grant: tap Grant. Your mic closes. Theirs opens.
  • ❌ To refuse: tap Decline. You keep the floor.

To get the mic back:

  • 🔄 Either the participant taps Release mic
  • ↩️ Or you tap Take the floor back

In both cases:

  • 🎤 A green banner shows: "You have the floor!"
  • 🔴 A red "MIC ON" indicator lights up
  • 💬 You can speak
⭐ Golden rule

Only one mic is open at a time.
Either yours, or one participant's.
Never both at once.
This keeps the subtitles clear.
