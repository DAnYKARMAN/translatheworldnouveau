Your first session adventure… in 9 steps!
1
DING!
You get your email!
After you sign up, TTW sends you an email with 2 links:
🔑 your HOST link (keep this one private!)
👥 your PARTICIPANT link (for your guests).
Click "Start the session" to begin 🚀
2
SETUP!
Set up your session
On the "Set up your session" page, fill in:
• Your name or nickname
• Your organization (optional)
• Your language (the one you'll speak)
• The audience languages (subtitles your guests will see).
Then click "Start session" 🚀
3
TADAAA!
Your control interface
This is the interface where you'll speak and run your conference: start it, allow (or decline) participants to speak, and end it.
You also see the room code, the countdown timer, and your connected guests in real time.
Now it's time to invite your participants!
4
⚡ DON'T MISS THIS ⚡
CLICK ON
"INVITE"!
Top right of your dashboard, the big turquoise button. THIS is what opens the menu to invite your participants (QR code, link, email, WhatsApp…).
5
✋ HAND!
A participant raises a hand
A participant wants to speak up?
They click the "Raise hand" ✋ button from their screen.
You (the host) get a notification.
Your call: accept or decline!
6
CHOOSE!
You allow… or not
A notification bar appears on your host screen.
✅ Allow: the participant can speak, their voice will be translated for everyone.
❌ Decline: they'll have to wait their turn.
You stay in full control of the session 🎯
🤫
⚠️ IMPORTANT ⚠️
The AI needs a QUIET environment
Translation works by capturing your voice. If the mic also picks up background conversations, clinking cutlery, music or a fan… the quality drops dramatically (poor transcription, poor translation).
❌ Avoid: noisy restaurants, busy cafés, open-plan offices, moving cars, crowded terraces.
✅ Ideal: closed meeting room, quiet office, headset mic placed close to your mouth.
7
🎤 THE MOST IMPORTANT 🎤
CLICK ON
THE MIC!
This is THE button that starts everything.
Without this click, no sound is captured and no translation appears for your participants.
👉 Click "Speaker" and just speak normally.
8
MAGIC! ✨
And there you go!
Here's how your participants follow your conference: the live translated subtitles appear right on their phone screen, big and clear, in their own language. 🌍
Well done! You're now a TTW pro 🏆
